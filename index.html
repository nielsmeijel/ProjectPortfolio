About

Getting to know me

As a Data Governance Senior Specialist at Valcon, I work with organizations across industries to establish and mature their data governance capabilities. My approach combines strategic thinking with hands-on technical expertise, ensuring that governance initiatives deliver real business outcomes.

I specialize in designing data quality frameworks, implementing metadata management solutions, and developing data catalogs that make information accessible and trustworthy. Whether it's defining data ownership models, establishing quality metrics, or building self-service analytics capabilities, I focus on practical solutions that scale.

My background in data science and analytics gives me a unique perspective on governance—I understand both the technical requirements and the end-user needs that good data governance should support.
